## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Prepare the following:
- A valid email address for registration
- Government-issued ID (passport or national ID card) for verification
- An EU bank account supporting SEPA transfers for funding
- Minimum initial deposit of €50 (check current requirements)

Ensure you are in an eligible country for Lendermarket services.
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and complete identity verification.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit the [Lendermarket investor portal](https://app2.lendermarket.com/sign-up).

    Fill in your email, create a password, and agree to the terms.

    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="edit-3">
    Log in and navigate to your profile settings.

    Enter personal details including full name, date of birth, address, and phone number.

    Upload a selfie if prompted for initial checks.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Identity" icon="shield-check">
    Go to the verification section in your dashboard.

    Upload clear photos of your ID document (front and back) and a proof of address (bank statement or utility bill, less than 3 months old).

    Lendermarket reviews submissions within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use high-quality images without glare for faster verification. EU residents typically verify quickest.
</Callout>

## Fund Your Account

Deposit funds via SEPA bank transfer, the primary method for secure funding.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Bank Transfer Details" icon="banknote">
    Log in to your dashboard and select "Deposit Funds" to view your unique IBAN.

    | Field          | Value                          |
    |----------------|--------------------------------|
    | Recipient Name | Lendermarket UAB              |
    | IBAN           | LT12 3456 0000 1234 5678      |
    | BIC/SWIFT      | AABALT22                       |
    | Reference      | Your Account ID (e.g., USER123)|
    | Amount         | Minimum €50                    |

    Initiate the transfer from your bank app or online banking. Funds appear in 1-3 business days.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Supported Currencies" icon="globe">
    EUR only for SEPA transfers. No credit cards or crypto accepted.

    Confirm your bank's SEPA compatibility to avoid delays.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Make Your First Investment

Once funded, invest in P2P loans for passive income.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Loans" icon="search">
    Navigate to the "Available Loans" section.

    Filter by return rate (up to 18% AAR), duration (1-5 years), and loan originators.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select and Invest" icon="shopping-bag">
    Choose a loan (e.g., €100 minimum).

    Review details: borrower rating, collateral, expected return.

    Click "Invest" and confirm the amount.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Progress" icon="trending-up">
    View your portfolio in the dashboard.

    Monitor auto-invest settings for ongoing opportunities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert" title="Investment Risks">
Past performance (15.58% average AAR) does not guarantee future results. Investments involve risk of loss. Diversify across multiple loans.
</Callout>
