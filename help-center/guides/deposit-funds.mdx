## Add Money to Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Lendermarket">
    Use your email and password at [lendermarket.com](https://lendermarket.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Deposit">
    Go to "Account" > "Deposit Funds".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select deposit method">
    Choose bank transfer or card payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow instructions">
    Complete the deposit steps. Funds usually arrive within 1-2 business days for bank transfers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
