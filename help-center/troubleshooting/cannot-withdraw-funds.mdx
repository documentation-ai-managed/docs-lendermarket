---
title: Cannot Withdraw Funds
description: Troubleshoot withdrawal issues on Lendermarket.
---

## Symptoms

- Withdrawal button is disabled
- Error: "Withdrawal unavailable"

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify your account">
    Ensure all required KYC documents are uploaded and approved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your balance">
    Confirm you have enough available funds for withdrawal (excluding invested or pending amounts).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If the issue persists, email support@lendermarket.com for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
