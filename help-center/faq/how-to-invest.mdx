## Overview

You can invest in loans directly through your Lendermarket account.

## Steps to Invest

1. Sign in to your Lendermarket account
2. Top up your account balance
3. Go to the "Loans" section
4. Browse available loans
5. Click "Invest" on a loan of your choice
6. Enter the amount and confirm

## Tip

<Callout kind="info">
  You can enable auto-invest for hands-free investing.
</Callout>
