## Overview

Lendermarket provides a regulated peer-to-peer (P2P) lending platform where you invest in diversified loans to earn passive income. You benefit from predictable returns, zero commission fees, and robust investor protections. The platform automates investments through features like auto-invest, helping you build a high-yield portfolio effortlessly.

With over `€38M` earned by investors and an average annual return of `15.58%`, Lendermarket stands out for its transparency and reliability on a EU-authorised crowdfunding service provider.
